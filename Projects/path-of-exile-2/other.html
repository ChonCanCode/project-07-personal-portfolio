Ascendancy

Warbringer

The Warbringer ascendancy class mainly capitalizes on ancestral spirits and their powers. It makes ancestral spirits more prevalent in combat, with them having more chances of spawning and longer duration times from skills such as Answered Call. Aside from ancestral summons, the Warbringer can tap into spiritual powers that provide him with more buffs and new abilities. An example would be the Jade Heritage skill, granting players a new armor skill that improves the Warrior's defensive capabilities. Unnamed Marauder - Ancestral proxies and Ancestor Totems
